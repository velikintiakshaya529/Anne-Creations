Anne Creations HB — Embroidery Designs Website

A static and fully responsive landing page developed for Anne Creations HB, a leading embroidery designs company in South India. The website is designed to showcase embroidery collections, highlight featured products, and provide essential company information in a clean and user-friendly layout.

Project Overview :

. Project Type: Static Website
. Technologies Used: HTML5, CSS3
. Main File: index.html
. Stylesheet: css/stylesheets.css
. Assets Folder: image/ (logo, icons, product images, social media icons)

Key Features :

. Top header with contact information (phone, email) and social media icons
. Responsive navigation bar with logo, menu links, search bar, and quick-access icons (account, wishlist, cart)
. Hero banner section showcasing embroidery highlights
. Marquee section displaying special offers
. Shop by Categories section (Butterfly, Peacock, Elephant, Flower, Jewellery, Gods)

New Arrivals product grid with:

. Product ID
. Price details
. Wishlist and cart icons
. View details option
. Weekly Best Sellers section featuring original and discounted pricing
. Structured footer with:
. Company description
. Quick links
. Support links
. Contact information
. Social media links

. Footer credit: Design & Developed by W3 Technologies

Highlights :

. Clean and structured HTML layout
. Responsive design using CSS Flexbox and Grid
. Mobile-friendly interface
. Well-organized folder structure
